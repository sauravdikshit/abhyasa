🗑️ Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to erasure under GDPR / CCPA.

How to permanently delete your HabitFlow data

You have two ways to request full deletion of your account and all associated personal data:

  • In-app: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This instantly removes your data.
  • Email request: Send a message from your registered email address to:
privacy@abhyasa.app

Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
Include your account email and (optional) user ID. We will process within 30 days and send a confirmation.

✉️ Request via Email

What gets deleted

All personal information: email, hashed password, habit history, streaks, reminders, and usage analytics. Anonymized aggregates may be retained for analytical purposes (no personal identifiers).

For any questions, see our Privacy Policy.
