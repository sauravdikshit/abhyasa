1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve HabitFlow:

  • Account data: Email address, username, and hashed password (we never store plain passwords).
  • Habit tracking data: Habits, streaks, completions, and custom reminders you create.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app — no personal identifiers attached.
  • Device info: OS version, device model, and app version for troubleshooting.

We do not collect sensitive data like precise location, contacts, financial information, or browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us deliver a seamless habit-tracking experience:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To sync your habits across devices.
  • To send optional reminders and motivational insights (you can disable these at any time).
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Period.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption: AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only. We regularly audit our security practices and update them as standards evolve.

While no system is entirely invulnerable, we take every reasonable precaution to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted providers to power HabitFlow:

  • GitHub Pages – hosts our public legal pages (may log IP addresses for security).
  • Supabase / Firebase – authentication and real-time database infrastructure.
  • Sentry – error tracking using anonymous crash data only.

Each provider complies with GDPR and CCPA where applicable. We evaluate third-party tools carefully and limit data shared with them to the minimum necessary.

5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (EU, California, and others), you have the right to:

  • Access – request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification – correct any inaccurate information in your account.
  • Erasure – delete your account and all associated data.
  • Restrict processing – limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Data portability – receive your data in a common machine-readable format.
  • Object to processing – opt out of processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, see the Data Deletion & Requests section below.

7. Cookies & Tracking

HabitFlow uses essential cookies only — for authentication sessions and user preferences. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics cookies that profile you across sites. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect login functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

HabitFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will remove it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email. The "Last updated" date at the top shows when this policy was last revised.

Continued use of HabitFlow after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. If you disagree with any changes, you may delete your account at any time.
