Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: June 9, 2026

Your privacy matters at HabitFlow. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve HabitFlow. This includes:

  • Account data: Email address, username, and hashed password (we never store plain passwords).
  • Habit tracking data: Habits, streaks, completions, and custom reminders you create.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app (no personal identifiers).
  • Device info: OS version, device model, and app version for troubleshooting.

We do not collect sensitive data like precise location, contacts, or browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us deliver a seamless habit‑tracking experience:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To sync your habits across devices.
  • To send you optional reminders and motivational insights (you can disable these).
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry‑standard encryption (AES‑256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit). Access is restricted to authorized personnel only. While no system is 100% invulnerable, we regularly audit our security practices.

4. Third‑Party Services

We use trusted providers to power HabitFlow:

  • GitHub Pages – hosts our public legal pages (logs IP addresses for security).
  • Supabase / Firebase – (example) for authentication and real‑time database.
  • Sentry – error tracking (anonymous data only).

Each provider complies with GDPR/CCPA where applicable. You can review their policies via our Subprocessors page.

5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (e.g., EU, California), you have the right to:

  • Access – request a copy of your data.
  • Rectification – correct inaccurate information.
  • Erasure – delete your account and all associated data.
  • Restrict processing – limit how we use your data.
  • Data portability – receive your data in a machine‑readable format.

To exercise any right, use the Data Deletion Request section below or contact us directly.

🗑️ Data Deletion & Requests

If you wish to delete your account and all personal data, or make any other privacy request, please email us from your registered email address:

privacy@habitflow.app

Include the subject line: "Data Deletion Request" or "Privacy Request" – and mention your account email. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation once completed.

You may also delete your account directly inside the HabitFlow app: Settings → Account → Delete Account.

6. Cookies & Tracking

HabitFlow uses essential cookies only for authentication and preferences. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect login functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

HabitFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Material changes will be notified via in‑app notification or email. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of HabitFlow after changes constitutes acceptance.
