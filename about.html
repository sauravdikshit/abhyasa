📖 Our Story

HabitFlow started as a personal project — a simple way to track daily habits without the noise of social features, gamification overload, or subscription paywalls. The developer behind it kept forgetting to drink water, skipping meditation, and falling off exercise routines. Every app felt either too complex or too juvenile.

So we built something different: a minimalist, privacy-first habit tracker that treats users like adults. No streaks lost to a missed day of WiFi. No ads selling you productivity supplements. Just clean, honest tracking that keeps you accountable to yourself.

Today, HabitFlow is used by over 50,000 people across 80+ countries — all through word of mouth.

💛 What We Stand For

🔒

Privacy by Default

Your habit data is deeply personal. We encrypt everything, never sell data, and give you full control to delete it at any time.

✂️

Radical Simplicity

Features should reduce friction, not add it. Every UI decision is questioned — does this actually help the user build habits?

🌱

Progress Over Perfection

A 5-day streak after a missed day is still amazing. We celebrate consistent effort, not flawless records.

🆓

Genuinely Free

Core habit tracking is free forever. No "premium" streaks, no locked insights. If we add paid features, the free tier stays powerful.
